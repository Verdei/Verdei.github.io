The Tale of Veronica Oleson

Born and raised in Omaha, NE and attended Northwest Highschool. I went to culinary school at Metro Community College and graduated with an Associates in Science. In August of this year I proudly graduated from the University of Nebraska at Omaha. I obtained a Bachelors in Business Administration with a Concentration in Management. Majority of my classes were in entreprenuership and I love the start-up scene we have in Omaha.

I've visited a dozen different countries over the last few years and inspire others to get out and travel. Traveling builds your soul and helps you grow and be so thankful for everything that we are blessed with here in the Silicon Prarie.

I will be a graduate of Interface Web School, where I learned the skills and ability to create this website. I tell people to never stop learning and keep growing; I improve and grow my skills every single day.
