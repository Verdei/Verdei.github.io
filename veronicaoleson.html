My Story

I was born and raised in Omaha, NE and come from a family of twelve kids; I fall fifth from the top of the order. I graduated from Northwest High School. I attended Culinary Arts School at Metropolitan Community College and graduated with an Associate of Applied Science. In August of 2016, I proudly graduated from the University of Nebraska at Omaha. I obtained a Bachelors Degree in Business Administration with a Concentration in Management. The majority of my classes were in entrepreneurship and I love the start-up scene we have in Omaha.

I worked on a cruise ship in Hawaii for six months and gained an urge to travel. I learned so much and the experience helped me grow. I've visited a dozen different countries over the last few years and inspire others to get out and travel. Countries I've visited: Ireland, England, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Vatican, Isreal, Belize, Mexico, Hungary and Honduras

I'm a graduate of Interface Web School, where I learned skills and ability to create this website. I tell people to never stop learning and keep growing; I improve and grow my skills every single day.

Gallup Entrepreneurship Strengths are as follows: Knowledge/Disruptor/Risk/Relationship. My Gallup Entrepreneurship Style is Relational.
