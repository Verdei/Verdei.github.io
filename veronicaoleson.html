My Story

I was born and raised in Omaha, NE and graduated from Northwest Highschool. I attended culinary school at Metropolitan Community College and graduated with an Associates of Science. In August of 2016, I proudly graduated from the University of Nebraska at Omaha. I obtained a Bachelors Degree in Business Administration with a Concentration in Management. The majority of my classes were in entreprenuership and I love the start-up scene we have in Omaha.

I've visited a dozen different countries over the last few years and inspire others to get out and travel.

I'm a graduate of Interface Web School, where I learned skills and ability to create this website. I tell people to never stop learning and keep growing; I improve and grow my skills every single day.

Gallup Entreprenuership Strengths are as follows: Knowledge/Disruptor/Risk/Relationship. My Gallup Entreprenuership Style is Relational.
